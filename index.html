Constitution

NEMSS 2014 Set Constitution

Article I: Purpose and Vision

The Student Memory Hub exists to create a lasting digital archive of our collective experiences, preserve meaningful memories, and foster connections among members through shared stories and thoughts. We aim to build a community where every memory matters and every voice is heard.

Article II: Membership

Membership is open to all students who share our values of respect, inclusivity, and community building. Members commit to active participation, regular contributions, and upholding the dignity of this shared space. Good standing requires fulfillment of financial obligations and respectful engagement with fellow members.

Article III: Meetings and Gatherings

Regular meetings shall be held monthly to discuss community matters, share memories, and strengthen bonds. Special gatherings may be organized for celebrations, remembrances, and community service. All members will receive adequate notice of meetings and are encouraged to attend and participate actively.

Article IV: Financial Responsibilities

Members agree to contribute monthly dues and participate in approved contribution campaigns for mutual support. All financial records shall be maintained with transparency and reported regularly to the membership. Special levies may be approved by majority vote for specific purposes benefiting the community.

Article V: Rights and Privileges

Every member has the right to contribute their memories, thoughts, and perspectives to the community archive. Members enjoy equal access to all community resources, voting rights on important matters, and the privilege of receiving support from fellow members during significant life events.
